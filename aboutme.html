About Me

Jael

Jael is my middle name. Jael was a courageous woman in biblical times who acted against the enemy and set the victory in the favor of her people. As an immigrant, I feel identified with this woman because we also fight courageously against the inequality of race,color,language,and gender.

Goals

My father used to say: "In life you never walk backwards, you always walk forward". One of my goals consist on building a career to service the community. We live in an era where technology can be a push of one button but the resources are still limited to the ones who could benefit from it the most. Ideally, one accomplishment would be to work on a project which is accesible to everyone worldwide.


Setting the Stage

I worked in the medical field for many years as a Monitor Tech. However, it is time to find a real passion. I recently started a coding boot camp at Rutgers Univeristy with the goal of working as Web Developer in the near future.
